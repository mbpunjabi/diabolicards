The Guide

Rules and reference. Keep it concise, scannable, and illustrated.

Setup

  1. Shuffle the deck and deal N cards to each player.
  2. Place the remaining deck in the center as the draw pile.
  3. Reveal the top card to start the discard pile.

Turn Structure

  1. Draw one card.
  2. Play one card or pass.
  3. Resolve effects and end turn.

How to Win

Reach X points first, or be the last player standing depending on your chosen play mode.
