The gist

  • 80 unique cards split across 11 groups—some regular, some diabolical, and some that can scorch the earth.
  • Play 1v1 or 2v2. Use cards and zone placement to build, steal, or eliminate power.
  • The game ends when no legal plays remain; the side with the highest power wins (ties stand).
Turn types
Choose either a Usual Turn (play a card, use abilities) or—if allowed—a high‑risk Contract Turn.
Three zones per side
Vask Tower • The Gang Hideout • Havenvale Campus. Slots and links matter for abilities.

About the world

After an apex virus, the Vantheon falls. Factions fight for control while Deathrender and Razor clash.
